## Short Questions

1. In one sentence, what did you think Scholr is for?
2. Which module felt most useful: Research, Notes, Doubt, or Documents?
3. Did the output help you understand or revise faster?
4. Was anything confusing on the page?
5. Did any answer feel wrong, unsupported, or hallucinated?
6. If you uploaded a PDF, were the citations/snippets useful?
7. Would you use Scholr before an internal exam or viva?
8. What subject would you try next?
9. What is one feature you expected but did not find?
10. Would you recommend this to a classmate? Why or why not?
